FAQ

  • How to buy
    • Install Metamask For Chrome Open your Google Chrome and visit metamask.io. Download the MetaMask chrome extension and set up a wallet. On mobile? Get MetaMask's app for Iphone or Android.
    • Add the Binance SmartChain Network to your wallet. Use the Instruction on the Binance Academy.
    • Transfer $BNB to MetaMask
      Transfer $BNB to your new MetaMask wallet from your existing wallet (e.g Trust Wallet, Coinbase, or Binance), or buy $BNB directly within Metamask.
    • Connect To Pancakeswap
      The safest place to buy $CatBoy is on Pancakeswap.
    • Swap $BNB for $CatBoy
      Set your slippage to minimum 12-15% and enter and the amount of $BNB you would like to swap for $CatBoy.
